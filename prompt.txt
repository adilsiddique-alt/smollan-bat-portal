If the display has NO people, NO competitor products, NO clutter, fully working lights, straight photo framing, and full stock, but shows minor neatness flaws, DO NOT REJECT. Mark it as APPROVED, set the score to a 6, 7, or 8 out of 10, and issue a friendly coaching nudge:
